TWICE is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. They debuted on October 20, 2015, with their first extended play (EP) titled "The Story Begins" and the title track "Like OOH-AHH." The group consists of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.
